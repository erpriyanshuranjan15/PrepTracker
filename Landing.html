FAQ

Questions? We got you.

Everything you need to know about PrepTracker before you start.

What exactly is PrepTracker and who is it for?+
PrepTracker is a web-based placement preparation platform designed specifically for engineering and tech students preparing for campus and off-campus placements. If you have interviews coming up and want a structured, data-driven way to prepare across DSA, System Design, aptitude, soft skills, and company research ‚Äî PrepTracker is built for you.
Is PrepTracker free to use?+
Yes! PrepTracker's core features ‚Äî skill tracking, daily goals, company pipeline, notes, and basic analytics ‚Äî are completely free. We believe every student deserves access to quality preparation tools regardless of budget. Premium features like AI-powered resume scoring and advanced analytics are available in our upcoming Pro plan.
How is the Readiness Score calculated?+
The Readiness Score (0‚Äì100) is a weighted aggregate of multiple factors: your technical skill levels (DSA, System Design, CS fundamentals), aptitude and soft skill scores, daily goal completion rate over the past 30 days, consistency streak, and company-specific preparation percentages. Each component is weighted based on its typical importance in placement interviews.
Can I track preparation for multiple companies simultaneously?+
Absolutely. The Companies module is specifically designed for this. You can add as many companies as you want, set their status (Preparing ‚Üí Applied ‚Üí Interview ‚Üí Offer), track individual prep progress per company, add company-specific notes and focus topics, and see your entire pipeline visualized as a funnel chart.
Does my data persist between sessions?+
Yes. PrepTracker uses your browser's local storage to persist your goals, notes, and other data between sessions. In the full backend version (built with Node.js, Express, and PostgreSQL via Drizzle ORM), all data is stored in a proper database and synced across devices when you're logged in.
What file formats are supported for resume upload?+
PrepTracker supports PDF and DOCX formats for resume uploads, with a maximum file size of 5MB per upload. The platform automatically creates a version history so you can always go back to a previous version, download any version, or set a new default. PDF files render directly in the preview panel.
Can I use PrepTracker on mobile?+
Yes! PrepTracker is fully responsive and works on mobile and tablet devices. The sidebar collapses into a hamburger menu, layouts adapt to smaller screens, and all charts and interactions are touch-friendly. We recommend using it on desktop for the full dashboard experience, but mobile works great for checking goals and notes on the go.
